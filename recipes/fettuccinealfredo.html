Fettuccine Alfredo

Fettuccine Alfredo is a simple pasta with minimal ingredients. This pasta is accompanied with a rich, creamy alfredo sauce made out of parmesan and cream. This pasta goes great with chicken, mushrooms, or shrimp.

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine Pasta
  • Butter
  • Garlic Clove
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Pepper
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Heat lightly salted water to boiling in a large pot
  2. In a large skillet, heat butter over medium heat. Then add minced garlic and cook for 2 minutes. Next, stir in heavy cream
  3. Let cream cook for 5 minutes, then add half your parmesan cheese. Whisk until cheese is fully melted
  4. Toss alfredo sauce with pasta
  5. Garnish pasta with the rest of your parmesan and parsley
