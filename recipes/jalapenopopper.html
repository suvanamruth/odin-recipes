Jalapeno Poppers

Jalapeno poppers are quick, delicious snacks to make when you're hungry. They're cheesy, spicy, and easy to make. This snack goes eprfect with garlic aioli, ranch, and queso

Ingredients

  • Cream Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Bacon Bits
  • Jalapeno Peppers
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Mix cream cheese with cheddar cheese and bacon bits
  2. Cut jalapeno peppers in half and scoop out insides
  3. Put milk, flour, and bread crumbs each in different bowls
  4. Dip the stuffed jalapenos in milk, then flour. Let dry for 10 minutes
  5. Dip jalapenos in milk once again and then dip them in the breadcrumbs
  6. Heat Oil to 365 degrees F in a skillet
  7. deep fry stuffed jalapenos until golden brown
