Fettuccine Alfredo

Chocolate Chip Cookies are the perfect baked treats everyone loves. These sweet deserts are perfect for cold days when you're stuck inside. These cookies go well with milk.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Brown Sugar
  • White Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix flour, baking soda, and salt
  3. Combine vegetable oil with brown sugar and white sugar. Then beat in eggs one at a time
  4. Stir in vanilla extract into the wet mix
  5. Combine wet mix with dry flour mix
  6. Fold in chocolate chips
  7. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes in the oven
